Tips For Beginners

  1. You do not have to write a lot - even a few minutes a day can make a difference.
  2. You can adapt journaling to fit your style. Try free writing, bullet journals, gratitude logs, art journaling, or even a combination of them all.
  3. There is no right or wrong way to do it - find what works for you.
