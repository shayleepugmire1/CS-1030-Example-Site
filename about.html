Journal Prompts

Free Writing Prompts

  • Start writing with: “Today, I want to talk about...” and don’t stop for 10 minutes.
  • Describe your current mood without using the words "happy," "sad," or "tired."

Bullet Journal Ideas

  • Make a list of things that make you smile.
  • Track your mood for the past week and note any patterns.

Gratitude Log Prompts

  • Write down three things you’re grateful for today—and why.
  • Who is someone that made your life better this week?

Art Journaling Prompts

  • Draw how you felt this morning vs. how you feel now.
  • Create a visual collage of your current dreams or goals.

Words of Affirmation

  • I am learning to trust my path.
  • My voice and feelings matter.
